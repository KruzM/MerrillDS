Nuvia Directory

This project involved building a fully custom internal website and resource directory for the lab division of a multi-million dollar dental implant company. Developed in Zoho Creator, the platform was designed to centralize tools, documents, and daily resources needed by employees across multiple lab locations. A key feature of this build was a custom Location Verification System developed in-house. This security measure authenticated users based on their physical location, ensuring compliance with internal policies by restricting access to authorized zones only.

Due to HIPAA regulations and internal company policy, a live preview of the platform is not available. However, a sanitized version of the source code is accessible here: Nuvia Directory

CCL Pro

In 2025, we completed a comprehensive digital presence package for a local landscaping company. This included a fully custom-coded website designed from the ground up to match the client’s specific vision and brand identity. The project also featured in-depth SEO optimization, helping the business rank higher in local search results and attract new leads organically. Additionally, we handled a complete social media setup—creating and branding accounts across major platforms, optimizing bios and contact links, and developing a content strategy to help the client engage with their community. Since launch, the project has proven to be a strong success, driving increased visibility, customer inquiries, and overall growth for the client.
